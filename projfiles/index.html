• Place Food Objects with left click; right click to remove them
  • Scroll to change their size
  • Select the parameters for the fungus
  • Press the Place Fungus button to place it in the centre of the screen
  • Press Play to start the simulation

  • The fungus will grow from the centre of the screen, and attempt to find the woodblocks
  • When the fungus encounters a woodblock, it will colonize it, and re-explore the environment
  • As the fungus grows, it will regress branches that are unconnected to food
  • The branches of the fungus which have found food will be strengthened
  • After some time, all that will be left is the network that connects the fungus to each food source it found
